Clinic Types Available for Lease

Clover Tower offers a variety of clinic types tailored to meet the needs of different medical practices. Explore the options below to find the right space for your clinic.

Boutique Clinics

Premium and private clinics located on the top floors of Clover Tower. Designed for upscale practices and consultants seeking exclusivity, luxury, and panoramic views.

Floors 1–3 Clinics

Ideal for cosmetic, dermatology, and dental clinics. These floors are high-traffic zones with larger front entrances and optimal patient access.

Floors 4–8 Clinics

Fully equipped clinics suitable for a wide range of medical specialties. Offers modern layouts and patient-friendly circulation.

Floors 9–17 Clinics

Spacious and customizable. Ideal for growing practices that need flexibility and additional room for staff and equipment.

Floors 18–19 Clinics

Executive-level clinics offering enhanced privacy, premium finishes, and elevated views of Kuwait. Perfect for established consultants.
